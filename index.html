A pencil sketch is a powerful expression of creativity where light and shadow come together to form a compelling visual narrative. As an artist, MJ uses delicate pencil strokes to capture not just the physical form of a subject but the essence of its mood and presence. The pencil, whether soft or hard, offers an incredible range of textures and tones, allowing for intricate detail and subtle gradations.

A "pencil sketch" can refer to a physical drawing made with a graphite pencil or a digital application that creates sketches from photos. Physical sketches are a traditional art form used for preliminary ideas or finished works, while apps offer a quick and easy way to create digital "pencil sketches" in various styles.
